ABOUT

Harboring beauty on the corner of Bali, Marina Dewata provides you a world-class Marina to fulfill your luxurious lifestyle, as a new way to spend your leisure with elegant serenity.
‍
On its 18 acres area in Klungkung, Bali, Marina Dewata indulges you with Public and Private Yacht dock area, classy resort and living facilities, as well as outstanding Marina facilities for utmost leisure and entertainment.
‍
Yachting and staying in the largest Marina Project in Southeast Asia now is not a mere dream. Live it.

LOCATION

Klungkung, Bali

Klungkung once a place where Balinese fought for their land, known as “PUPUTAN KLUNGKUNG”. Although the aggression does not end with victory. The new ruler decided to appoint a king, which is none other than Ida I Dewa Agung Gede Oka Geg the Klungkung king himself.

NATURE RESERVE

Connect with Mother Earth

Preserving the environment is our priority. Marina Dewata is meant to be inseparable from nature. Therefore, we help to preserve nature spots to protect the environment from further harm.

Visitors to Marina Dewata are able to relish the nature reserve in Klungkung, from sightseeing the greenery to exploring the mystery of nature.

(01)
Forest Trail
Walk or jog between the trees to mingle with nature. The cleared path allows you to stride deeper into its beauty.
(02)
Coastal Footpath
The sun bath, fresh air, and mesmerizing view of the sea will accompany you when wandering the coastal footpath.
(03)
River Walk
Walking alongside the river is another way to celebrate the charm of nature. Stop for a while to feel how the fresh water runs along your feet and hands.
(04)
Forest Pavilion
Built around plant life, the forest pavilion is an excellent choice for small events, as well as an aesthetic photo background.
(05)
Eco-Leisure Park
Visit the preserved nature in Klungkung in the family-friendly eco-leisure park. Delightful moments await you.
