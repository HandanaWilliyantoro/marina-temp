ABOUT

Harboring beauty on the corner of Bali, Marina Dewata provides you a world-class Marina to fulfill your luxurious lifestyle, as a new way to spend your leisure with elegant serenity.

On its 18 acres area in Klungkung, Bali, Marina Dewata indulges you with Public and Private Yacht dock area, classy resort and living facilities, as well as outstanding Marina facilities for utmost leisure and entertainment.

Yachting and staying in the largest Marina Project in Southeast Asia now is not a mere dream. Live it.

LOCATION

Klungkung, Bali

Klungkung once a place where Balinese fought for their land, known as “PUPUTAN KLUNGKUNG”. Although the aggression does not end with victory. The new ruler decided to appoint a king, which is none other than Ida I Dewa Agung Gede Oka Geg the Klungkung king himself.
MARINA FACILITIES

Sails of your dream.

This is where your journey starts. Experience the charming vistas and fresh breezing air when you sail on a yacht from Marina Dewata, to beyond.

Your journey wouldn’t be ordinary. Among the sea of beauty, you can taste an unforgettable dining experience, starting from local specialty to molecular gastronomy cuisines. During the day, you can also enjoy a yacht showcase, boat show, even join a sailing championship. At night, fireworks display and fun events will await. Right here, there won’t be a wasted time.

Yatch Club

Celebrate the sport of yacht in the passionate yacht club. Meet new friends, join fun activities together, and gain many benefits in Marina Dewata Yacht Club.

Active Broadwalk

A walk is not just a walk when the view is wonderful, the shops are lively, and the sounds of harboring yachts singing in your ears.  Even just walking in Marina Dewata will give you an amazing experience.

Active Waterfront

Marina Dewata transforms Klungkung seaside into an active recreational waterfront. Being here, you can choose to take a walk, enjoy sunset and sunrise, shop, dine, or do it all!

Celebrate the sport of yacht in the passionate yacht club. Meet new friends, join fun activities together, and gain many benefits in Marina Dewata Yacht Club.

Berthz

Marina Dewata has more than 300 berths with Superyacht and Megayacht berths, making it the largest Marina in Southeast Asia.

Sailing School
